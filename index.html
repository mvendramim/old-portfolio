About the App

Exposure is an app to help you better expand your knowledge. You can take a picture of text and Exposure will turn the words into text that you can search. Choose to search from Google, Serendipomatic, Bing, dictionary.com, Amazon, Google Translate and Google Images. If you would like to do an image search, take a picture of anything! Exposure will let you search for similar images in Google Images, Bing Images and Flickr. If you’re confused about a word you read in your novel, a menu item at a restaurant, or even the meaning of a street sign, Exposure is here to help. There are endless possibilities. Exposure is a new way of searching, what will you find out?

How it Works

1. Take a picture

When you see words that you would like to look up, take a picture of the text and Exposure will convert the picture into text for you. If you would like to search an image, just choose image search .

2. Edit the text

When you see words that you would like to look up, take a picture of the text and Exposure will convert the picture into text for you. If you would like to search an image, just choose image search .

3. Search

Choose to search your text in Google, Serendipomatic, Bing, dictionary.com, thesaurus.com, Google Translate or Google Images.

4. Learn

We hope you can find the answers that you’re looking for. If not, pick a new search engine and try again!
